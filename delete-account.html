Account Deletion Guide

Step-by-step instructions to delete your SewSmart account

Important Information

Deleting your account is permanent and irreversible. By proceeding, you will lose:

  • All saved measurements and client data.
  • All active "stitches" (orders) and tracking history.
  • Your community posts and profile visibility.
  • Active premium subscriptions (no refunds provided).
1
Open the SewSmart app on your device and ensure you are logged in.
2
Navigate to the Profile or Settings tab (usually represented by a gear or user icon).
3
Scroll to the bottom of the Settings page and tap on the red "Delete My Account" link.
4
A confirmation dialog will appear. To verify your identity, type your registered email address into the input field provided.
5
Once the email matches, tap the red "Delete Account" button to finalize the process. Your data will be wiped from our servers immediately.
