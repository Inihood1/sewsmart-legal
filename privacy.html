Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 27, 2026

Welcome to SewSmart. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and profile picture.
  • Business Information: If you are a tailor, we collect your business name and address to help customers find you.
  • Customer Data: Measurements and order details (stitches) that you save within the app.
  • Location Data: We use your GPS location to help you find nearby tailors or to show your shop to local customers.
  • Community Content: Photos, descriptions, and comments you post in the community feed.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service.
  • To facilitate social interactions and "Order This Style" connections.
  • To help users discover nearby tailoring services via location data.
  • To manage subscriptions and premium features.
  • To notify you about changes to our app or responses to your inquiries.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information in the following cases:

  • Public Content: Your posts and business profile are visible to other users in the community.
  • Direct Contact: When a user taps "Order This Style", your contact information (WhatsApp/Phone/Email) is shared with them to facilitate the order.
  • Service Providers: We may use third-party companies (like Firebase) to facilitate our Service.

5. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to access, update, or delete your information at any time. You can delete your entire account and all associated data (including measurements, orders, and posts) directly within the app settings.
